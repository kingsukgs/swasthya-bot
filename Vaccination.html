Vaccination Schedules

Stay safe with timely vaccines for infants, children, and adults.

By Age Group

Infants (0–12 months)

  • BCG at birth
  • Polio (OPV) at 6, 10, 14 weeks
  • DTP at 6, 10, 14 weeks
  • Hepatitis B at birth, 6, 10 weeks
  • Measles at 9 months

Children (1–10 years)

  • MMR at 15 months
  • DTP booster at 18 months, 5 years
  • Polio booster at 18 months, 5 years
  • Typhoid vaccine at 2 years

Adolescents & Adults

  • Tdap booster every 10 years
  • HPV vaccine (for teens)
  • Flu vaccine yearly
  • COVID-19 vaccines as per guidelines

Vaccines for Specific Diseases

COVID-19

  • Primary series as per age group
  • Booster doses as per guidelines
  • Updated vaccines for new variants

Rabies

  • Post-exposure prophylaxis after dog/animal bite
  • Pre-exposure for high-risk individuals (vets, lab workers)

Hepatitis A & B

  • Hepatitis A: 2 doses, 6 months apart
  • Hepatitis B: Birth dose, then 6 & 10 weeks
  • Boosters as needed
