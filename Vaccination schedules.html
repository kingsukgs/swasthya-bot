Vaccination Schedules

Track timely vaccines and reminders for all age groups.

Infants (0–12 months)
  • BCG at birth
  • Polio (OPV) at birth, 6, 10, 14 weeks
  • DTP at 6, 10, 14 weeks
  • Hepatitis B at birth, 6, 10 weeks
  • Measles at 9 months
Children (1–10 years)
  • MMR at 15 months
  • DTP booster at 18 months, 5 years
  • Polio booster at 18 months, 5 years
  • Typhoid vaccine at 2 years
Adolescents & Adults
  • Tdap booster every 10 years
  • HPV vaccine (for adolescents)
  • Flu vaccine annually
  • COVID-19 vaccines as per government guidelines
